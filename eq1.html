Math Questions & Solutions

Q1: Write the sets in roster form

(i) \( A = \{ x : x \text{ is an integer and } -3 \leq x < 7 \} \)

(ii) \( B = \{ x : x \text{ is a prime number which is a divisor of 60} \} \)

Solution:

(i) \( A = \{-3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6\} \)

(ii) Prime divisors of 60: \( B = \{2, 3, 5\} \)

Q2: Write the sets in set-builder form

(i) \( A = \{2, 4, 8, 16, 32\} \)

(ii) \( B = \{1, 4, 9, 16, 100\} \)

Solution:

(i) \( A = \{x : x = 2^n, n \in \mathbb{N}, 1 \leq n \leq 5\} \)

(ii) \( B = \{x : x \text{ is a square of a natural number}\} \)

Q3: Venn Diagram Questions

(i) Draw \( A - B \)

(ii) Draw \( A' \cup B' \)

Solution:

(i) \( A - B \) means elements in \( A \) but not in \( B \).
\( A - B = \{3, 6, 9, 15, 18, 21\} \)

(ii) \( A' \cup B' \) means complement of \( A \cup B \). The result is the area outside both sets.

               /           \

            X  \         /   X
                 \_______/
           A' ∪ B': Outside both circles

Q4: Find complements

(i) \( X = \text{even natural numbers} \)

(ii) \( Y = \{x : x + 5 > 8\} \)

Solution:

(i) Complement of \( X \) (natural numbers not even): All odd natural numbers.

(ii) Simplify \( x + 5 > 8 \): \( x > 3 \). Complement is \( x \leq 3 \).

Q5: Find \( A \times A \) if \( A = \{1, -1\} \)

Solution:

Cartesian product \( A \times A \): All ordered pairs from \( A \).

A × A = {(1, 1), (1, -1), (-1, 1), (-1, -1)}

Q6: Let \( R \) be the relation on \( A = \{2, 3, 4, 6\} \)

\( R \) is defined as \( (a, b) : a \text{ divides } b \).

Solution:

Check divisibility:

R = {(2, 2), (2, 4), (2, 6), (3, 3), (3, 6), (4, 4), (6, 6)}
